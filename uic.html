Welcome to UIC

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1905

Welcome to the University of the Immaculate Conception (UIC), where faith, excellence, and service converge to create transformative educational experiences. Whether you're here as an international visitor, exchange student, or intern, we're honored to welcome you into our UIC family.

Founded in 1905 by the Religious of the Virgin Mary (RVM), UIC stands as the first Catholic school in Davao City. Over more than a century, we have evolved from a primary school into a prestigious university, receiving University Status in 1992.

Our Core Values:

  • Faith: Nurturing spiritual growth and Catholic teachings
  • Excellence: Providing top-quality education for future success
  • Service: Instilling values of humility, love, justice, and peace
