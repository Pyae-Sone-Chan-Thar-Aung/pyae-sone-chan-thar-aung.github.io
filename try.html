About Me

I am a young Burmese youth; an enthusiastic tech addict and a problem-solver. Justice and the minor ethnic groups that reside in the mountainous regions of Myanmar are deep interests of mine, and I like to advocate for them through Information and Communication Technology. I am pursuing my undergraduate degree, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at the University of the Immaculate Conception, Davao City, the Philippines. I was born and raised by a loving family. My dad is a skillful car driver, and my mom is a dedicated janitor. I have two elder sisters and I am the youngest in the family. I grew up in a mountainous area, far from our relatives. Now, I am living in Davao City, Philippines.
